electron-is-dev 环境端口

concurrently 链接多个执行命令
eg: concurrently \"npm start\" \"electron .\"

wait-on
eg: concurrently \"npm start\" \"wait-on http://localhost:3000 && electron .\"

cross-env 设置跨平台的环境变量
eg: concurrently \"cross-env BROWSER=none npm start\" \"wait-on http://localhost:3000 && electron .\"

electron-store 数据持久化
